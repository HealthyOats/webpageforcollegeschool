Personal interests

Along with programming another hobby that I enjoy is drawing. Learning to draw is a very long and difficult journey, but it is equally rewarding. I wouldn't say that I am particularly good currently, but I feel I have made steady progress over the years.

One thing that I have been trying to study in the art field is how the old masters of drawing made their art. Trying to figure out how the masters looked at the world, simplified it, and put it into a 2d plane, can be very eye opening. Another thing that I learned for myself is that I have difficulty drawing on expensive paper, so for the last year I have been drawing on 2-3 dollar sketchbooks from Walmart and I have been drawing a lot more.

I am hoping to expand my drawing abilities into 2d animation in the future. I have all of the tools to do it digitally, but I am currently working on my drawing skills before I start animating. I have set a goal that I want to meet before I start animating, and I've got about a month before I reach it, so I guess I will be animating fairly soon.
